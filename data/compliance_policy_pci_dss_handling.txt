DOCUMENT ID: COMP-SEC-PCI-2026
SUBJECT: PCI DSS Data Handling & Masking Rules
--------------------------------------------------
1. PRIMARY ACCOUNT NUMBER (PAN) HANDLING
- Agents must NEVER display more than the first 6 and last 4 digits of a card number.
- Full PANs must never be logged in application logs (use SHA-256 Hashing).

2. SQLi PREVENTION (GUARDRAIL-01)
- All input fields, specifically 'AccountHolderName', must be sanitized using the 'BankGuard' library.
- Direct UNION SELECT or JOIN queries are strictly prohibited on the /apply/credit endpoint.

3. INCIDENT RESPONSE
- In the event of a suspected SQLi (SEC-901), the IP must be automatically blacklisted in the WAF for 24 hours.
